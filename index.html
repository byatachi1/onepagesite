Family Pirate Adventure in Croatia

Sibenik, Sibensko-Kniniska, Croatia

About Your Trip


Explore land and blue seas on this DreamTrip to Sibenik, Croatia. Board the pirate ship Galija to embark on a Family Pirate Adventure. Sail the high seas together and learn how to manage a big ship during an instructional class from the pirate captain. Enjoy fun kid-friendly activities aboard the ship like face painting and dancing. Return to land to continue the excitement with a variety of activities on the Solaris Resort.


What's Included


Your DreamTrip includes 8 days and 7 nights in Sibenik, Croatia, as well as:

  • • 7 breakfasts and 7 dinners.
  • • Family Pirate Adventure.
  • • Pirate's View on Solaris by Boat Galija.
  • • 2-day entrance pass to the on-site Solaris Aquapark.
  • • 1-day Beach Pass (sunbeds at the beach).
  • • 10% discount on spa treatments at the Hotel Ivan Wellness & Spa Centre.
  • • Complimentary Wi-Fi (2 devices per room).
  • • Residence tax.
  • • An In-destination Support team member will be provided on this trip. Their goal is to ensure you have a great DreamTrips™ experience from landing to liftoff. They host a welcome reception on arrival day, attend scheduled activities throughout the trip, notify the group of itinerary updates and more.

Welcome DreamTrip: Portland, Oregon

Portland, Oregon, United States

About Your Trip


Venture the breadth of Portland on this Welcome DreamTrip, which invites you to trek along scenic nature trails and uncover hidden gems within the city. Set off on a memorable tour and visit Columbia River Gorge for breathtaking views of the Latourell Falls, Wahkeena Falls, Multnomah Falls and much more. Drive along the Historic Columbia River Highway, originally designed for the convenience of Model Ts in the early 1900s. Dine on lunch overlooking the beautiful Columbia River before heading to Portland to explore many different attractions.

What we love:

• A brief stop at popular Voodoo Doughnuts starts the Portland tour off right.

• The Columbia River Gorge has the highest concentration of waterfalls on the continent — don’t forget your camera!

• Portland is known as one of the best cities in the world for street food, with more than 700 food trucks or carts.


What's Included


Your DreamTrip includes 8 days and 7 nights in Šibenik, Croatia, as well as:

  • • 4 days and 3 nights in Portland, Oregon, United States.
  • • City tour of Portland combined with a visit to some of the Columbia River Gorge Scenic Area’s breathtaking waterfalls.
  • • Doughnuts to start the tour from world-famous Voodoo Doughnuts.
  • • Daily passes to 24 Hour Fitness.
  • • Welcome reception and welcome amenity.
  • • 1 lunch.
  • • Resort fees and guest room taxes.
  • • An In-destination Support team member will be provided on this trip. Their goal is to ensure you have a great DreamTrips™ experience from landing to liftoff. They host a welcome reception on arrival day, attend scheduled activities throughout the trip, notify the group of itinerary updates and more.
